What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator helps you estimate your daily calorie needs to maintain, lose, or gain weight. Based on factors like your weight, height, age, gender, and activity level, it calculates how many calories you should consume every day. This tool is essential for understanding your energy requirements and achieving your health or fitness goals efficiently.
